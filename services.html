Our Services

Radiotherapy Treatments

A treatment that uses x-ray scans that radiates the tumor bed which can take up to a few days or weeks.Book appointments with our qualified doctors for general health and specialist consultations.

Chemotherapy Treatments

A treatment that kills all cancer cells in the body that is given according to the patients cancer type or stage. The treatment can be done orally or intravenously. Patients are granted access to prescription medicines, over-the-counter drugs, and professional pharmacy advice. Book appointments with our qualified doctors for general health and specialist consultations.

Hormonal Therapy

This treatment is reserved hormone sensitve cancers like breast and prostate cancer. The treatment is a tablet that can be taken daily or monthly injections given by the neurosurgeons team. Book appointments with our qualified doctors for general health and specialist consultations.

Biological treatments

These are considered to be immunotherapies which are either stimulated intravenously or orally. Book appointments with our qualified doctors for general health and specialist consultations.

Supportive Treatments

Treatments that support the patients immune system from cancer cells in the body. Book appointments with our qualified doctors for general health and specialist consultations.
